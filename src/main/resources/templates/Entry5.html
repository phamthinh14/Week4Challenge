The Circles of Program Design

SDLC or the Software Development Life Cycle is a process that produces software with the highest quality and lowest cost in the shortest time. SDLC includes a detailed plan for how to develop, alter, maintain, and replace a software system. SDLC involves several distinct stages, including planning, design, building, testing, and deployment. The most popular SDLC model is the Agile model.
